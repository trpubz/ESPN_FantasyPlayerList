The official start of the 2022 fantasy baseball season may be in a bit of a limbo right now, but that doesn't mean you can't start the process of figuring out which players you may want to draft for your teams. The following rankings are designed specifically with ESPN head-to-head category leagues and rotisserie leagues in mind.

Please note that these rankings reflect what we think players will do in the upcoming season and not how they may have performed in previous campaigns. We will update these rankings once the current labor situation comes to a conclusion or as major news warrants.

A full ESPN Draft Kit will be released in the coming weeks, so stay tuned. In the meantime, sign up for ESPN Fantasy Baseball today!

Note: Tristan H. Cockcroft's points league rankings can be found here.
